Health expenditure data from the WHO Global Health Expenditure Database (GHED), 2016 onwards.

- country_name: Country or territory name as used by WHO
- iso3_code: ISO 3166-1 alpha-3 country code
- year: Year of observation
- spending_purpose: Health care function or purpose of spending (Curative care, Rehabilitative care, Long-term care (health), Ancillary services, Medical goods, Preventive care, Governance and health system administration, Other health care services)
- pct_current_health_exp: Spending as a percentage of current health expenditure (CHE)
- usd_2023: Spending in constant 2023 US dollars
